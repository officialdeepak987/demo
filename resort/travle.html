Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the check-in and check-out time?

+

Check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may be arranged upon request at no extra charge for loyalty guests.

Do you offer airport transfers?

+

Yes! Complimentary airport pickup is included for bookings of 3 nights or more. Private transfers with luxury vehicles can also be arranged for an additional fee.

Are meals included in the room rate?

+

Breakfast is included with all our standard room bookings. Full board (breakfast, lunch & dinner) is available as an add-on and is included in all package deals.

Can I customize a tour package?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in fully bespoke travel experiences. Contact our concierge via WhatsApp or the enquiry form and we'll craft a personalized itinerary based on your preferences, budget, and travel dates.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Free cancellation is available up to 7 days before check-in. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% charge. No-shows are charged the full amount. Package bookings may have different terms — please enquire directly.
