FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've answered the most common ones below. For anything else, just WhatsApp us!

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
How do I book an appointment?▼

You can book via WhatsApp at +91-99999-99999, call us directly, or use the online form on this page. We confirm your slot within 15 minutes.

Do you accept insurance?▼

Yes! We accept major health insurance plans including Bajaj Allianz, Star Health, HDFC ERGO, MediAssist and government schemes like CGHS and Ayushman Bharat.

What are your clinic timings?▼

We're open Monday–Saturday 8 AM to 9 PM and Sundays 9 AM to 2 PM. Emergency consultations are available outside hours — call us.

Can I get my lab reports on WhatsApp?▼

Absolutely! All lab reports and prescriptions are digitally sent to your WhatsApp within the promised turnaround time — usually 2–4 hours for most tests.

Do you offer home visits or home sample collection?▼

Yes — we offer home blood sample collection for select lab tests. Home visits are available for senior patients in specific cases. Contact us via WhatsApp to arrange.

Is parking available at the clinic?▼

Yes, free parking is available for all patients in our dedicated parking lot. The entrance is from Medical Lane, and the parking can accommodate 40+ vehicles.
