Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment before visiting?

While we do accept walk-in patients depending on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment via our website or WhatsApp to minimize waiting times.

What are the OPD timings across both locations?

Noida Sector 22 branch remains open 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Monday–Saturday). Our Noida Extension branch is open for evening OPD from 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM (Monday–Friday).

Do you offer diagnostic services at the clinic?

Yes, we provide essential diagnostic services including digital X-rays, ultrasound, bone density scanning, and various laboratory blood tests.

Does Dr. Deepika provide infertility treatments?

Yes, Dr. Deepika specializes in pre-conception counseling and comprehensive infertility evaluation and treatments to support your parenthood journey.
