Founder & Chief Physician

Dr. Akash Garg, MBBS, MD (Medicine), is the founder of Dr. Akash Garg Clinic. He is deeply committed to providing affordable healthcare to every patient without compromising on quality.

Located at B 5, Sector 22, Noida (Opposite Noida Stadium, Near Adobe), our clinic offers comprehensive medicine consultation, pathology laboratory services, and indoor pharmacy. Dr. Garg is known for his patient-centered approach and professional, honest consultation.
