Our Story

We Are More Than
Photographers

Born from a deep love for storytelling and the magic of human connection, Lumière Films was founded in 2012 by Arjun & Priya Mehta — a husband-wife duo who believed that every wedding deserves to be remembered like a cinematic masterpiece.

With over 850 weddings captured across 28 cities, we've become synonymous with artistry, emotion, and timeless elegance. Our team of 12 passionate creatives brings a documentary-meets-cinematic approach to every frame we capture.
